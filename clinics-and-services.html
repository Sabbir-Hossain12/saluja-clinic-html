Browse Our Clinics

Select any clinic below to learn more about what we offer and how to access it.

Core Service
Surgery Clinics
Access our regular GP surgery clinics for consultations, follow-ups, and general medical care. Available Monday to Friday with both face-to-face and telephone appointments.
Paediatrics
Children's Health
Dedicated paediatric consultations, immunisations, developmental reviews, and child health surveillance from birth through to adolescence.
Long-Term Conditions
Chronic Diseases
Ongoing management and monitoring of long-term conditions including diabetes, asthma, COPD, hypertension, and cardiovascular disease.
Procedures
Minor Surgery
In-practice minor surgical procedures including skin lesion removal, cyst excision, and wound care performed by our experienced GPs.
Immunisation
Vaccinations
NHS vaccination programme including flu, COVID-19 booster, childhood immunisations, travel vaccines, and pneumococcal jabs.
Screening
Cancer Screening
NHS cancer screening referrals and advice covering bowel, breast, cervical, and prostate screening for eligible patients.
Women's Health
Women's Health
Comprehensive women's healthcare including contraception, cervical smears, menopause management, and gynaecological concerns.
Men's Health
Men's Health
Prostate health, sexual health, mental wellbeing, weight management, and preventive care tailored to men's specific health needs.
Preventive
NHS Health Checks
Free NHS Health Check for patients aged 40–74, assessing your risk of heart disease, stroke, kidney disease, and type 2 diabetes.
Family Planning
Planning a Family
Pre-conception advice, fertility concerns, antenatal referrals, and guidance on starting or growing your family safely.
Wellbeing
Healthy Lifestyle
Support for smoking cessation, weight management, alcohol advice, physical activity, and improving your overall wellbeing.
Specialist
Anti-Coagulation
Regular INR monitoring and warfarin dosing clinic for patients on anticoagulation therapy, ensuring safe and effective management.
Private
Non-NHS Work
Private medical reports, letters, DVLA examinations, insurance reports, and other non-NHS services. Fees apply — contact reception for details.
Administration
Sickness Certificate
Information on fit notes (sick notes), Med 3 certificates, and self-certification for short-term illness. Available from your GP when clinically appropriate.

Not sure which service you need?

Our reception team are happy to guide you to the right clinic. You can also book an online consultation and your GP will direct you to the most appropriate service.
