What You Can Do

Everything in One Place

The NHS App brings together the services you need most — securely, simply, and available from your smartphone around the clock.

Book Appointments

Request GP appointments directly through the app — without waiting on hold. Depending on availability, you can also manage, reschedule and cancel upcoming appointments.

Order Prescriptions

Order your repeat prescriptions in seconds and nominate your preferred pharmacy for collection. Track your prescription status — and get a notification when it's ready.

View Health Records

Access your full GP health record including test results, medications, allergies, conditions and immunisations. Your medical history, always in your pocket.

Check Test Results

View your blood test results, letters and documents from your GP — often before they arrive by post. Stay informed about your health between appointments.

NHS 111 Online

Access NHS 111 directly through the app for urgent medical advice at any time of day or night — without needing to call. Get directed to the right care, fast.

Manage for Family

Parents and carers can manage healthcare services for their children and loved ones through the app — making it easy to keep the whole family's health organised.

Why Use the NHS App

Healthcare on
your terms

The NHS App lets you take control of your health — no phone queues, no waiting rooms for routine admin, no paperwork. Everything your practice offers digitally, in one secure place.

Available 24 hours a day Order prescriptions at midnight, check results on your lunch break. The app is always on, even when the surgery is closed.

Getting Started

Up and running in
four steps

Setting up the NHS App takes just a few minutes. You will need your mobile number and a form of photo ID to verify your identity.

1
Download the App

Search for "NHS App" on the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android) and download it for free. It also works in your web browser.

2
Create an NHS Login

Register with your email address and mobile number. Verify your identity using a photo ID such as a passport or driving licence.

3
Link to Your GP

The app will automatically connect to your Saluja Clinic record. You must be aged 13 or over and registered with an NHS GP in England.

4
Start Using It

Book appointments, order prescriptions, view your health records and more — all from your phone, any time you need.

Already using Systm Online?

You can continue to use Systm Online exactly as you do now — nothing changes. The NHS App is an additional way to access your services and you can use both side by side. There is no need to switch if you prefer Systm Online.

Privacy & Security

Built on NHS-grade security

NHS Login — Verified Identity
Your account is protected by NHS Login, which verifies your identity using official photo ID. Face ID and fingerprint login are supported for daily convenience.
End-to-End Encrypted
Your health data is transmitted and stored with end-to-end encryption. The NHS App is developed and maintained by NHS England to the highest data security standards.
You Control Your Data
The NHS App lets you manage your own data sharing preferences. You decide what is shared, with whom, and when — including national data opt-out settings.
