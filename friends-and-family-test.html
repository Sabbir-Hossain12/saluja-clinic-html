Why Feedback Matters

Your Voice Shapes Our Care

The NHS Friends and Family Test (FFT) is a feedback tool that helps us understand whether patients are happy with the care they receive, so we can continuously improve our service.

Results are published monthly on this page and submitted to NHS England. Your responses are completely anonymous and take less than a minute to complete.

We review every piece of feedback — including comments — at our regular clinical governance meetings to drive real, measurable improvements.

Your feedback is anonymous. We do not collect any personal information as part of the Friends & Family Test. Your responses cannot be traced back to you.
What happens with your feedback
1

Collected Monthly

Anonymous responses are gathered throughout the month from patients who have recently attended the practice.

2

Reviewed & Discussed

Results are reviewed at our clinical governance meetings with all clinical and administrative staff.

3

Published & Reported

Monthly results are published here and submitted to NHS England as required by our NHS contract.
