At Saluja Clinic, we are committed to continuously improving how our patients access their GP services. As part of our 2025/26 Access Improvement Plan, we have set out clear, measurable targets designed to make it quicker, simpler, and more convenient for you to reach us — whether by phone, online, or in person.

Our Commitments to You

We know that accessing your GP practice should be straightforward. This plan reflects our honest assessment of where we are today and the specific steps we are taking to improve.

How We're Improving Access

We have introduced a number of practical changes to help meet these targets and to make your experience with the practice as smooth as possible.

📞

Improved Telephone Access

We have reviewed our telephone system and staffing to reduce waiting times. Phone lines open Monday to Friday, 08:00–18:30.

🌐

Online Consultations via WebGP

Our online consultation service is available 24 hours a day at salujaclinic.webgp.com. Submit your query at any time and we'll respond by the next working day.

📱

NHS App Registration Drive

We are actively encouraging all patients to register for the NHS App to book appointments, order repeat prescriptions, and view records online.

💊

Streamlined Repeat Prescriptions

Repeat prescription requests can be submitted online, reducing the need to call the practice for routine medication.

📋

Regular Progress Reviews

We will review performance against these targets regularly and share updates through this website and our Patient Participation Group.

Your Feedback Matters

We value the views of all our patients and encourage you to share your experiences. If you have feedback on how we are delivering against this plan — positive or constructive — please contact the practice or complete our Friends and Family Test.

Our Patient Participation Group meets regularly and welcomes patient involvement in shaping how services are delivered. If you'd like to get involved, please speak to our reception team.
