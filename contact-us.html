How to Get in Touch

There are several ways to reach us. For medical matters use phone or online consultation; for admin queries, email is fine.

Call the Practice
Speak directly to our reception team for appointments, prescriptions, test results and medical questions. Open Mon–Fri 8:00am–6:30pm.
📞 0208 574 5136
Online Consultation
Submit a consultation request at any time via our online system. We aim to respond by the next working day. Ideal for non-urgent medical questions.

Visit Us in Person
Come in during opening hours and our reception team will assist with appointments, forms, and general enquiries. No appointment needed for admin.

Non-Emergency
NHS 111

Available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year — free from all landlines and mobiles. For urgent medical advice when your GP is closed.

Life-Threatening Emergency
999

Dial 999 immediately for chest pain, breathing difficulties, loss of consciousness, severe bleeding, or any life-threatening condition.
