We are delighted to welcome you to the brand new Saluja Clinic website — designed with you, our patients, at the heart of everything. After careful planning and development, we are proud to launch a site that makes accessing your GP practice easier, clearer, and more convenient than ever before.

Designed Around Our Patients

Our previous website served us well, but we recognised that the needs of our patients have changed. More of you are accessing services online, using mobile devices, and expecting information to be available quickly and clearly. This new site has been built from the ground up with those needs in mind.

Whether you want to book an appointment, request a repeat prescription, find out about our services, or simply get in touch — everything is now just a few clicks away.

What's New

Alongside the refreshed visual design, you'll find a wealth of new and updated content across the site — detailed information on all our clinics and services, a comprehensive Health Information section, updated practice policies, and a full Meet the Team page.

We've also made it much simpler to access key tools — including the NHS App, online consultations via WebGP, and information for new patients looking to register with the practice.

Try Our Online Consultation

Submit your query online and we'll respond by the next working day.

We'd Love Your Feedback

This website is a living resource and we plan to keep it updated with the latest news, health information, and service updates. If you have any feedback — what you love, what could be improved, or anything you'd like added — please don't hesitate to get in touch with the practice team.

We hope you find the new Saluja Clinic website a genuinely useful part of managing your healthcare. Thank you for being part of our practice community.
