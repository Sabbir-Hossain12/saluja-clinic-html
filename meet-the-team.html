Mrs Balbir Saluja
Practice & Business Management
Wife and lifelong business partner to Dr Rajinder Singh Saluja, Mrs Balbir Saluja has been integral to the practice since its foundation. Together they opened Arog Medical Centre in 1986, relocating to the current Northcote Avenue site in 1997 to establish Saluja Clinic. A qualified AMSPAR Practice Manager, Mrs Saluja served as both Practice Manager and Business Manager throughout the practice's formative decades — simultaneously overseeing operations, governance, and the day‑to‑day wellbeing of staff and patients, whilst managing the family's Nursing Home Portfolio and coordinating clinical support for Dr Rajinder during his Forensic Medical work with Scotland Yard. She continues to provide guidance and oversight on a daily basis, working closely with her son Dr Angad Saluja, and with our current Practice Manager, Amarjeet Bhambra — who also serves as the INT Managerial Lead for the PCN.
