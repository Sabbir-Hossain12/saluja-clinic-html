Nurse-Led Services

Clinics Provided
at Our Surgery

The following clinics and services are delivered here at Saluja Clinic by our nurses and healthcare assistants. To book, use our online system or call us on 0208 574 5136.

Adult Immunisations

Including Tetanus, Polio, Influenza and other recommended adult vaccinations. Delivered by our trained nursing team.

Advice on Diet, Smoking & Lifestyle

Personalised guidance on healthy eating, smoking cessation, alcohol awareness and positive lifestyle changes to support long-term wellbeing.

Health Checks — Ages 16 to 74

Routine health screening for adults aged 16–74, including blood pressure, BMI, cholesterol and lifestyle risk assessment.

Health Checks — Ages 75 & Over

Dedicated annual health reviews for patients aged 75 and over, including medication reviews, mobility checks and falls risk assessment.

Child Immunisations

Full NHS childhood vaccination schedule including 8-week, 12-week, 16-week, 12-month and pre-school booster programmes.

Family Planning Services

Confidential contraceptive advice and services, including the pill, contraceptive injections and referrals to specialist services.

Maternity Care

Antenatal and postnatal care including booking appointments, shared care with midwifery services, and new-born checks.

Oral Contraceptive Pill

Prescriptions and ongoing monitoring for the oral contraceptive pill, including reviews, blood pressure checks and advice.

Walk-In Triage Clinics

Same-day access for urgent but non-emergency concerns. Our triage nurse will assess you and direct you to the most appropriate care.

Well Baby / Child Clinic

Developmental checks, growth monitoring, feeding advice and early childhood health assessments for infants and young children.

Well Woman Clinic

Cervical screening, breast awareness, menopause consultations, and holistic women's health assessments for patients of all ages.
